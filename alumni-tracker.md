## How to fill rows 4-10 (15-20 min)

**Targeting criteria** (from the research):

- 3-8 years out: senior enough to refer, junior enough to reply
- PM or PM-adjacent title at the probable Purdue clusters: Amazon, Microsoft, Google, Walmart Global Tech, Capital One, JPMorgan, Salesforce, High Alpha / Indy tech
- MEM alumni are disproportionately PM-dense (MEM incubated the PM Club) — mine that sub-network
- Mix it: aim for at most 2-3 per company so one cold pocket doesn't sink the week

**Steps:**

1. Open `linkedin.com/school/purdue-university/people` (Alumni Tool)
2. "What they do" → Product Management. Intersect with "Where they work" → Google / Amazon / Microsoft etc.
3. Keyword box for what filters miss: `Product Manager`, `APM`, `Technical Program Manager`, `Product Analyst`
4. Repeat on the Purdue MEM page (`linkedin.com/company/purduemem`) and Daniels/Krannert sub-page

**Boolean queries** (paste into People search, add School = Purdue filter; operators must be UPPERCASE):

```
("Product Manager" OR "Associate Product Manager" OR APM) AND (Google OR Amazon OR Microsoft)
("Product Analyst" OR "Strategy and Operations" OR "Strategy & Ops")
("Technical Program Manager" OR "Program Manager") NOT recruiter
("Product" OR "Strategic Analyst") AND ("Capital One" OR JPMorgan OR "JP Morgan")
```
