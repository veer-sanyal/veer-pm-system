# LinkedIn Presence for a Student Aspiring PM

Companion to `Genuine_Networking_for_Competitive_PM_Internships.md` (DM outreach, reply-rate math, alumni blueprint). What to post; how the feed works. 3-vote adversarial-verification harness applied.

## Headline: trust the mechanics, not the numbers

Of 25 claims tested, **17 were refuted** — precise benchmarks are marketing-blog folklore.

**Refuted — do not plan around:** "best time/day" tables (Buffer, Hootsuite, Sprout all conflicting); carousel/video multipliers ("596% more", "5x video"); ideal character counts; "60% link penalty" + first-comment workaround; dwell-time weighting; comment/share/reply weight rankings; "15-min reply boost"; "21x profile views"; "one comment beats ten posts"; all outreach reply-rate benchmarks. Real numbers come from your own analytics.

## 6 verified findings

| Finding | Verdict |
|---------|---------|
| Recruiters/alumni look first on LinkedIn (Jobvite: 95–97% of recruiters use it). Profile not optional. | 3-0 |
| **Golden hour** — algorithm tests a post in the first **60–120 min**; early engagement expands reach. Post when you can personally seed early comments. | core 3-0 |
| **Consistency beats volume** — 1–2 quality posts/week beats burst-then-silence. Fixed weekly day. (1–2/week is a floor, not a ceiling.) | 3-0 |
| **Build-in-public beats promotional** — build-logs, teardowns, learnings, data viz perform best. Showing work substitutes for seniority. | 2-1 |
| **Comment to warm outreach** — substantive comments on target's posts for **1–2 weeks before** any cold note. Direct bridge to Pillar-3. | 3-0 |
| **Portfolio links in static fields only** — Featured/About carry no feed penalty; post bodies do. India MSME dashboard goes in **Featured + About**; no links in post bodies. | 3-0 |

## What to do

**Profile:** keyword-led headline; dashboard in **Featured** + **About** (first 2–3 About lines are the hook); use the banner; complete profile + free verification.

**Content (no title):** (1) build-logs incl. mistakes (hardcoded-10x-vs-real-29.4x story); (2) one chart + "what surprised me" — you already produce `chart_A`/`chart_B`; (3) PM product teardowns; (4) learning-in-public; (5) reflections tied to real action. **Specificity substitutes for authority.**

**Cadence:** 1–2 posts/week on a fixed day. Post when you have 60–90 min to reply — your presence in the golden hour beats any "optimal clock time" (all refuted).

**Engagement:** comment substantively (does NOT replace posting); reply to own comments; **skip engagement pods** (unproven upside, real authenticity/detection downside).

## Anti-patterns

Broetry; engagement bait ("Comment 'PM' below"); inspirational-fiction stories; pure self-promo (~80/20 value-to-promo rule); generic praise; links in post bodies; trusting any precise benchmark.

## Open questions (your analytics will answer)

Best times/days for your audience; true link-penalty size; which format performs; realistic warmed-up reply rate; engagement-pod risk/benefit. All published numbers refuted — measure your own.
