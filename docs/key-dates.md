### Important: Google APM closes earlier than I initially had it

For summer 2027, expect Google APM to close around **early-to-mid October 2026**. That means the prep timeline is tighter than originally framed. The shipped project, technical credibility, and resume v2 need to be in shape by **end of September 2026** at latest. Otherwise the application goes in weak.

The full-time new-grad APM is a post-graduation program, so for a 2028 grad it is a summer-2028 target, not summer 2027. ## Application timing strategy

The trap to avoid: waiting for a date that never comes. The pattern across companies in 2026:

- Microsoft: postings appear regionally throughout the year. Check monthly starting fall 2026.
- Google APM: fall application cycle. Apply early, rolling review. Late October close is the latest signal.
- Databricks, Uber Freight: open in fall, close before winter break. Apply quickly when posted.
- Snowflake: opens later (April). Don't assume early closure.
- Stripe Accelerator: spring close, but this is full-time new-grad, not summer intern.

The implication: from September 2026 forward, Veer should be checking the careers pages of his 8-10 target companies weekly. The application is sometimes the limiting factor — not preparation.

## What to do when the dates above turn out to be wrong

Some of these dates will shift. Programs will move, get cut, or change cycles. The rule:

- Once a month, Veer (or planner) checks the careers pages of all 8-10 target companies
- When a posting goes live for a target company, application happens within 2 weeks (not "when I'm more ready")
- The application is not the prep; the prep is what's been happening for 6+ months by then
